Barley and Red Lentil Curry with Chicken


Chicken, Barley & Red Lentil Curry is a rich, soul-warming dish that strikes the perfect balance between comfort food and bold flavor.
Tender chunks of chicken simmer in a fragrant blend of curry spices, garlic, and ginger, nestled into a creamy base of nutty pearl barley
and soft red lentils that practically melt into the sauce. Each spoonful is thick, hearty, and infused with warm aromatics—like your favorite takeout,
but more satisfying and deeply homemade. Finished with a splash of coconut milk for just the right touch of silkiness,this one-pot wonder is both
nourishing and craveable. Serve it up with fresh herbs, warm flatbread, or just a big spoon—it’s the kind of dish that hugs you back.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 3/4 cup pearl barley
  • 1/2 cup red lentils (rinsed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or ghee
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated or minced
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne (optional)
  • 1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups chickedn broth
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional, added after cooking)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley (optional garnish)
